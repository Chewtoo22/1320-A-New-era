## Opponent Progression
Players will face a variety of opponents as they progress through the tiers. The opponent difficulty will scale as follows:
- **Tier 1:** Basic opponents with minimal skills.
- **Tier 2:** Intermediate opponents with increased skills and strategies.
- **Tier 3:** Advanced opponents with challenging AI that adapts to player strategies.

## Prize Pools
The prize pools for each tier are structured to reward players based on their performance:
- **Tier 1:** 500 in-game credits for winning.
- **Tier 2:** 1,000 in-game credits for winning.
- **Tier 3:** 2,500 in-game credits for winning.

## AI Difficulty Scaling Mechanics
The AI difficulty will scale based on player performance metrics, including win ratios and average lap times:
- If the player wins consistently, the AI will become more aggressive and skilled.
- If the player struggles, the AI will relax its tactics to ensure a balanced competition.

This system aims to provide a challenging yet fair racing experience for all players, ensuring that each tier feels distinct and rewarding.
